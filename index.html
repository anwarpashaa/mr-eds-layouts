MR. ED’S MAGICAL MIDWAYS

Mr. Ed's Magical Midways is a premiere amusement ride supplier to fairs, festivals, and events throughout the state of Wisconsin. Additionally, we also rent rides, games, and food to movie production sets and large corporate functions. Not only do we provide some of the best rides in Wisconsin, but we also provide fair food, midway games, and even midway amenities to help make your event look like a permanent amusement park.

Our mission is to provide your event a state of the art carnival with safe rides, courteous employees, customer amenities and exceptional service. We strive to cultivate the highest possible level of amusement ride services through passionate, inspirational and innovative contributions to every project that we take part in.

Mr. Ed’s Magical Midways will continue to deliver top notch family entertainment without sacrificing what is up most important to the company-customer satisfaction. Mr. Ed’s Magical Midways will continue to be an industry leader with the talent and vision our company is so justly known for.

We look forward to bringing many smiles to your community.
